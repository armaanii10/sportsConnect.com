About Sports Connect

Sports Connect is a platform that connects college students with university pickup games in different sports. Whether you're looking to play basketball, soccer, volleyball, or any other sport, Sports Connect helps you find and join pickup games happening on your campus.

Key features of Sports Connect include:

  • Discover and join pickup games in various sports
  • Connect with other college students who share your sports interests
  • Organize and schedule pickup games with friends or fellow students
  • Stay updated with the latest pickup game events happening on your campus
  • Share your pickup game experiences and photos with the community

Join Sports Connect today and start playing your favorite sports with fellow students!
